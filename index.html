Soleia

Refreshing Sunflower-Scented Shampoo

Description

Soleia is a clear shampoo with a refreshing sunflower scent (Helianthus annuus). Specially formulated to gently cleanse and nourish your hair, leaving it soft and fragrant.

Key Details

  • Volume: 100ml
  • Scent: Sunflower (Helianthus annuus)

Directions for Use

  1. Apply evenly on wet hair.
  2. Massage gently into a lather.
  3. Rinse thoroughly.

Ingredients

  • Aqua
  • Sodium Lauryl Sulfate
  • Glycerin
  • Astragalus Gummifer Gum
  • Sodium Citrate
  • Methylparaben
  • Propylparaben
  • Hydrogenated Sunflower Seed Oil

Important Information

For external use only. In case of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with water.
